Hydra Crane & Crane Rental in Raichur — What You Need to Know

What is a Hydra Crane?

A hydra crane — also called a pick and carry crane — is a mobile crane that can carry a suspended load while moving, without outriggers or extensive setup. The hydraulic boom extends to lift the load, then the crane drives to the drop point. This makes it ideal for factory floors, warehouses, and construction sites where speed and mobility are critical.

Hydra Crane vs Mobile Crane — Which Do You Need?

A mobile crane is suited for large, heavy lifts at a fixed point — high-rise structural steel, bridge girders, or major one-time heavy loads. A hydra crane is better when you need to move a load across a site, work in confined spaces, or complete multiple lifts quickly. Most factory and warehouse projects in Raichur choose hydra cranes for exactly these reasons.
